# OSINT Integrity Checker

This skill provides the agent with advanced digital investigation capabilities, focusing on verifying identities and uncovering hidden connections across the open web.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Digital Footprint Analysis
Track and verify the presence of individuals or entities across hundreds of platforms.
- **Username Reconnaissance**: Use logic from `Sherlock` and `Maigret` to find profiles across social media, forums, and professional networks.
- **Domain/IP Investigation**: Analyze digital assets to uncover ownership and historical data.

### 2. Corporate Intelligence
Conduct deep-tier investigations into corporate entities.
- **Structure Mapping**: Identify parent companies, subsidiaries, and key executives.
- **Compliance Linkage**: Connect OSINT findings to AML/KYC risk profiles.

### 3. Identity Verification
Cross-reference data points to ensure the integrity of a digital identity.
- **Anomaly Detection**: Identify inconsistencies in public records or social profiles.
- **Evidence Gathering**: Collect verifiable digital evidence for audit reports.

## Procedural Knowledge

- **Operational Security (OPSEC)**: Ensure all investigations are conducted without alerting the target.
- **Data Privacy**: Adhere to ethical standards and legal constraints regarding public data collection.
